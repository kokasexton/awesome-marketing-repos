## 💬 AI Marketing Prompt Library

*Copy-paste ready prompts for Claude, ChatGPT, or any AI. Use these as starting points and customize for your context.*

---

**📝 Content Brief**
```
You are a senior content strategist. Create a detailed content brief for a [BLOG POST / VIDEO / NEWSLETTER] about [TOPIC].

Include:
- Target audience and their pain points
- Primary keyword and 5 semantic keywords  
- Recommended headline options (3 variations)
- Outline with H2 and H3 structure
- Key points to cover and angles to take
- CTA recommendation
- Word count and tone guidance

Company context: [YOUR COMPANY AND VALUE PROP]
```

---

**🔍 Competitive Analysis**
```
Analyze [COMPETITOR NAME] as a direct competitor to [YOUR COMPANY].

Research and summarize:
1. Their positioning and messaging (homepage headline, tagline, key differentiators)
2. Top 5 content themes and formats they use
3. Estimated traffic sources and channels
4. Pricing model and packaging
5. What they're doing better than us
6. Gaps and weaknesses we can exploit
7. One strategic insight we should act on immediately

Be direct and specific. No fluff.
```

---

**📧 Email Sequence**
```
Write a 5-email nurture sequence for [PERSONA] who just downloaded [LEAD MAGNET].

Sequence goals: move them from awareness to booking a demo.

For each email include:
- Send timing (Day X)
- Subject line (and A/B variant)
- Preview text
- Email body (conversational, 150–200 words)
- Primary CTA

Tone: [FORMAL / CONVERSATIONAL / BOLD]. 
Product: [PRODUCT NAME AND 1-LINE DESCRIPTION]
Key pain points to address: [LIST 2-3]
```

---

**🔎 SEO Blog Post**
```
Write a 1,500-word SEO-optimized blog post targeting the keyword "[PRIMARY KEYWORD]".

Requirements:
- Hook in first 2 sentences (no generic intros)
- H1 includes primary keyword naturally
- 4–5 H2 subheadings with semantic keywords
- At least one data point or statistic per section
- Internal link opportunities: [LIST YOUR RELATED PAGES]
- Conclusion with a clear CTA to [ACTION]
- Meta description under 160 characters

Target audience: [DESCRIBE READER]
Our unique angle: [WHAT MAKES OUR TAKE DIFFERENT]
```

---

**📣 Ad Copy Variants**
```
Generate 5 ad copy variants for [PLATFORM: Facebook / LinkedIn / Google] promoting [PRODUCT/OFFER].

For each variant:
- Headline (max 30 chars for Google, 70 for social)
- Primary text / description (max 125 chars for Facebook)
- CTA button text

Test angles: Pain-focused, Outcome-focused, Social proof, Urgency, Curiosity.

Product: [NAME]
Key benefit: [ONE SENTENCE]
Target audience: [DESCRIBE]
Offer: [FREE TRIAL / DEMO / DISCOUNT / ETC]
```

---

**💼 LinkedIn Post**
```
Write a high-engagement LinkedIn post about [TOPIC].

Format: Hook → Story or insight → Lesson or takeaway → CTA

Rules:
- First line must stop the scroll (question, bold claim, or surprising stat)
- No corporate jargon
- Short paragraphs (1–2 sentences max)
- End with a question to drive comments
- 150–300 words

My perspective / experience: [SHARE A PERSONAL ANGLE OR STORY]
Goal: [AWARENESS / LEADS / ENGAGEMENT / RECRUTING]
```

---

**🎯 ICP Profile**
```
Build a detailed Ideal Customer Profile (ICP) for [PRODUCT].

Include:
1. Firmographic fit (company size, industry, stage, revenue)
2. Technographic fit (tools and stack they likely use)
3. Trigger events (what makes them "in market" right now)
4. Champion profile (title, goals, day-to-day pain)
5. Economic buyer (who controls budget)
6. Disqualifiers (who is NOT a fit)
7. Where they spend time online

Product: [NAME AND DESCRIPTION]
Current best customers: [DESCRIBE 2-3 OF YOUR BEST ACCOUNTS]
```

---

**📅 Campaign Brief**
```
Create a full campaign brief for [CAMPAIGN NAME / GOAL].

Include:
- Campaign objective and success metrics (with targets)
- Target audience segment
- Core message and positioning
- Channel mix and rationale
- Content requirements per channel
- Timeline and key milestones
- Budget allocation recommendations
- What "winning" looks like at 30/60/90 days

Budget: [RANGE]
Timeline: [START - END DATE]
Primary KPI: [METRIC]
```
